About us:

Where We Are

Located in: Yeka Sub-city, Woreda 8, Addis Ababa

our campus spans over 9,000 m². It is designed to give students a calm, spacious, and inspiring environment for both learning and personal growth.

Facilities

Our school includes

  • modern classrooms
  • fully equipped science laboratories
  • a student clinic
  • creative art gallery
    • Each space is designed to support both academic excellence and student wellbeing.

Learning Beyond Classrooms

We offer separate libraries for primary and secondary students, creating focused environments for every age group. Our Scout program also provides outdoor learning experiences, including on-campus camping and teamwork activities.

Sports

Students enjoy football, basketball, volleyball, and ping pong through exciting tournaments and friendly matches. Whether competing seriously or playing just for fun, sports bring energy, teamwork, and school spirit to our community.

Science Laboratories

Our Physics, Chemistry, and Biology laboratories allow students to learn through practical experiments. Hands-on learning helps develop scientific thinking, curiosity, and problem-solving skills that extend beyond the classroom.

Computer & ICT Education

Technology plays an important role in modern education. Our computer laboratories provide students with opportunities to learn digital skills, programming, research, and responsible technology use in a safe environment.

Creative Arts

Creativity is an essential part of education. Students express themselves through drawing, painting, drama, music, and cultural performances throughout the academic year.


Student Safety & Wellbeing

The wellbeing of every student is our highest priority. We provide a safe campus, caring teachers, student guidance services, and a healthy learning environment where students can feel supported every day.

School Events

Throughout the year, students participate in educational trips, cultural celebrations, science exhibitions, sports competitions, talent shows, and community service activities that make learning enjoyable and memorable.
