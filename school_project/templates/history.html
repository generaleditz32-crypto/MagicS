OUR HISTORY

Discover the journey of Magic Carpet School from a small beginning to a modern institution committed to excellence.

The Magic Carpet Story

Chapter One - Foundation

Magic Carpet School was established in 1999 in Yeka district of Addis Ababa with the goal of providing quality education and nurturing future leaders.

The school began with a small community of students and dedicated teachers sharing a vision of academic excellence.

Chapter Two - Early Development

As enrollment increased, new classrooms were built, additional teachers joined the staff, and more subjects were introduced.

Major Milestones

New Subjects

Expanded curriculum for every learner.

Qualified Teachers

Experienced educators joined the school.

Campus Growth

Facilities improved to support learning.

Chapter Three - Academic Growth

Students consistently achieved stronger academic performance while science, ICT and extracurricular programs continued to expand.

Chapter Four - Modern Era

Today the school offers modern classrooms, laboratories, digital learning tools and a supportive environment for every student.
